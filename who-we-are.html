Profile - Chief Executive Officer

Taofik Kolawole Alimi (HND, Dip in Advertising, MBA)

Taofik is a multi talented and purpose driven Manager with more than a decade work experience at Senior and General Sales and Marketing Management, National Business and Channel Development, Route to Market Strategy Design, Trade/Shoppers Survey and insights and team lead management.

Amongst prominent position he has held successfully are; Regional Sales Manager West MidWest (Nestle) 2004-2008; Branch Manager North East (Nestle) 2008-2011; National Business Development Manager (Nestle) 2012-2013; and National Sales Manager (Nestle Waters) 2013- 2014 Nov)

On record is his achievement in Nestle where he set up business development structure with focus on Route to Market strategy, Coverage and retail market penetration.

He was a member of the project team dedicated to transformation of Sales operation in Nestle Nigeria in 2008.

Member of Nigerian Institute of Management and Member of Nigerian Institute of Marketing.
